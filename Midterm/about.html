About us!

Our mission is to offer outstanding quality ingredients for our array of customers. Ubatuba is a unique alternative to the ever-so-populating health food spots. Our fast and convenient service get you in and out quick and full stomach of healthier options. Throughout the years, we have perfected the recipes to get the best results when it comes to the featured items on our menu.

Our specialty item is our acai bowl that you can mix and match an assortment of our fresh ingredients. These are the super berries of today for your body’s health and we want you in on the magic! Ubatuba works hard to provide the best of the best for you and your friends or family. Let us brighten up your meal, we promise you won’t regret it and your body will be happy you did!

By now many people know that the Açaí berry is one of the most nutrient dense foods on the planet with a unique combination of protein, healthy oils, fiber, and antioxidants. Most people don’t know how important acai has become in protecting and preserving the Brazilian Amazon as the vast forest faces ever increasing pressure. Acai cultivation provides income for countless families living in the Amazon river basin and a sustainable alternative to the destructive practices of clear cutting, cattle grazing, and mining.
